Department of Law

Discover our extensive law programs designed to equip you with the necessary skills for a successful career in legal fields.

Courses Offered

Bachelor of Laws (LLB)

The LLB program provides a comprehensive understanding of legal principles and practices, preparing students for various legal professions and roles within society.

Master of Laws (LLM)

The LLM program is designed for legal professionals seeking to specialize in areas such as international law, human rights law, or corporate law.
