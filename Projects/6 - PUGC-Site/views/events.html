Past Events

5th Convocation - PUGC

1050 students from Business, Commerce, IT, and Law were awarded degrees. 18 students received gold medals. Vice-Chancellor Prof. Dr. Niaz Ahmad Akhtar praised the campus leadership, particularly Dr. Naveed Iqbal Ch.

Date: March 16, 2022

6th Convocation - PUGC

The chief guest was Vice Chancellor Prof. Dr. Asghar Zaidi, with 500 degrees awarded. The VC emphasized learning from Allama Iqbal’s life and leadership. Medals were awarded to top students, and faculty efforts were recognized.

Date: December 24, 2022

Seminar on Mental Health

Organized by the Department of English in collaboration with Learning Hub College. Key participants included Dr. Naveed Iqbal Ch and other departmental heads. Mr. Mussarat Nawaz Dogar played an essential role in organizing.

Date: 2023

Art Attack

A vibrant event with singing, painting, and drama competitions involving all six departments. Drama: 1st place – English Department. Singing: 1st place – Law Department. Painting: 1st place – IT Department.

Date: March 18, 2023
